About myself

Hi, I am Rumy! Some people call me "fox", "cute dog", "red dog from the Internet". But I'm a big, formidable dog, I am Shiba-Inu. And this is a story of my journey. Looking ahead, I will say that unexpectedly, I found myself in a different part of the globe.

I had a wonderfully peaceful life in Saint-Petersburg, Russia. Warm couch, delicious food, long walks with my man. By the way, I own four people: a pair of adults and two human puppies. I love my cubs, although sometimes I am ready to devour them. As for adults, well, I would like to learn how to open the frige myself to take my food, and then I can get rid of groun ups. Oh, and there's Sofina, big fluffy mouse of Pomeranian breed.

Anyway, as I said, I had a pretty good time in Saint-Petersburg…

Before the journey

But suddenly, at the end of February 2022, everything has changed. There was a feeling of depression, anxiety in the family.

Something unusual was happening. I was taken to the vet twice; they did tests for antibodies to the rabies virus and a test for leishmaniasis.

This went on for a while, and at the beginning of July, my people began to pack their bags. I thought we were going on vacation by car. We've done this before, it was a lot of fun. But this time there were containers for me and Sofina and my people did not rejoice in the coming journey. And then one morning my people loaded the suitcases into the car (and me and Sofina in containers) and went to the airport. I counted 6 suitcases, 4 hand luggage and 2 dog cages. It wasn't easy for my people to handle all our luggage, but they did it. Luckily, the driver and airport staff were very kind and helpful.

We went through all the formalities at the airport and to my displeasure, the airport workers took me and the Sofina and placed us in the luggage compartment of the aircraft.

On our way

So, my journey has begun. It was dark and noisy inside the plane’s hold. My people put some ice cubes in my watering can and I had water during the flight. We flew by Turkish Airlines, the flight was delayed by 1.5 hours, but all the rest went well and after 4 hours flight we arrived to Istanbul.

My man later said that he asked the flight attendant several times how the dogs were doing. I doubt this, probably he was just enjoying his flight.

My people left part of bags in the luggage room and we checked into a hotel near the airport. It was warm and good in Istanbul.

We spent two nights at the hotel and drove back to the airport. It turned out that in Istanbul there was only a long gap between the flights (40 hours).

After a short wait at the airport, Sofina and I were again placed in the luggage compartment of the aircraft. But this time the flight took 17 hours (with a stopover in San Paolo)!!!

So, late in the evening we arrived in Buenos Aires. Apparently, my man was tired from the flight, because he forgot one of our suitcases at the airport. Fortunately, he himself realized that the bag was missing and managed to pick it up. Finally, we took all our luggage and spent the night in the hotel in the center of Buenos Aires.

I was going to rest at the hotel, but my man explained that we needed to buy tickets for the ferry. It turned out that Buenos Aires was not the final destination of our trip.

The difficulty was that russian bank cards do not work abroad and you cannot buy tickets online. Therefore, we were looking for an ATM that works with the Chinese payment system Union Pay to withdrawal cash.

It appeared, my man did not rest enough, because he forgot his card in the ATM. Luckly, the girl who used the ATM after him caught up with him and returned the card back. In general, we met exceptionally good people along the way. This gives hope that it is not all lost in the human world.

Then we got to the ferry terminal and bought tickets for everyone. The weather was good, although it was winter in Argentina in July.

The next morning we boarded the ferry and in less than 3 hours we arrived to the capital of Uruguay – Montevideo.

Finally arrived!

Montevideo has welcomed us with clear blue sky, warm sun and calm sea. So, I ended up very far from home. Here my people will have to start all over again, completely reset their lives and careers. That's all for now. Peace for everyone.
